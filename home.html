About Us

College Consultancy is dedicated to helping students navigate the complex process of selecting tertiary institutions. We provide personalized consultations, college comparisons, scholarship guidance and application assistance. Our team consists of experienced education counselors and academic advisors.

Our Mission

To empower students with data-driven guidance so they can select colleges that match their academic strengths, career goals and budgets.

What we offer

  • Personal profile evaluation
  • College shortlisting
  • Application & essay support
  • Scholarship & financial aid guidance
